Demo Reader is a Chrome extension designed for [BRIEF_DESCRIPTION]. This privacy policy is specific to the Demo Reader extension and supplements our [umbrella privacy policy](index.md).

## Information Collection

### What We Collect

Demo Reader collects the following information:

- **User Settings**: Preferences you configure within the extension
- **Reading Progress**: Local tracking of your reading position (stored locally only)
- **Extension State**: Data necessary for the extension to function properly

### What We Do NOT Collect

- Personal identification information
- Your reading content or text
- Browsing history
- Data from other websites or services

## Data Usage

All data collected by Demo Reader is used solely for:

- Providing core extension functionality
- Maintaining your preferences and settings
- Improving user experience within the extension

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. No data is transmitted to external servers.

## Permissions

Demo Reader requires the following Chrome permissions:

- **Storage**: To save your preferences and settings locally
- **ActiveTab**: To interact with the current browser tab when you use the extension

These permissions are used exclusively for extension functionality and do not enable any data collection beyond what is described in this policy.

## Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to third parties. All data remains local to your browser.

## User Control

You can:

- Clear extension data at any time through Chrome's extension management interface
- Uninstall the extension to remove all associated data
- Modify or reset preferences within the extension settings
