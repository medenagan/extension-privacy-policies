## Information Collection and Use

Our extensions are designed with privacy in mind. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

### Data We Collect

Our extensions may collect and use the following types of information:

- **Extension Usage Data**: Basic usage statistics to improve functionality
- **User Preferences**: Settings and configurations you choose within the extension
- **Local Storage**: Data stored locally on your device for extension functionality

### Data We Do NOT Collect

- Personal identification information
- Browsing history outside of extension functionality
- Passwords or sensitive credentials
- Data from other websites or applications

## Data Storage and Security

All data collected by our extensions is:

- Stored locally on your device
- Not transmitted to external servers (unless explicitly stated in extension-specific policies)
- Protected using industry-standard security practices

## Third-Party Services

Our extensions do not integrate with third-party analytics or advertising services unless explicitly stated in an extension-specific privacy policy.
